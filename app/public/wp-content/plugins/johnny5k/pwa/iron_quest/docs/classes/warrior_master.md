# Johnny5k: IronQuest

## Warrior Class - Master Design Document (v1)

## Overview

The **Warrior** is the strength-focused class in IronQuest.

Warriors thrive on:

- Heavier weights
- Pushing beyond limits
- Dominating high-intensity encounters

They are designed for users who:

- Enjoy lifting hard
- Want visible performance-based rewards
- Are motivated by challenge and progression

## Core Identity

> "Power is earned. Push harder."

## Primary Playstyle

- Lift heavier than baseline
- Exceed rep targets
- Perform strongly in boss encounters

## Core Bonuses

| Bonus Type                  | Effect                                                      |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Exceeded set bonus          | +8 XP per exceeded set                                      |
| Heavier completed set bonus | +10 XP per heavier completed set, capped at +40 per workout |
| Boss impact                 | Stronger impact on boss outcomes                            |

## Base Stats

| Stat              | Value                              |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Max HP            | 120                                |

| HP loss tolerance | Slightly higher than other classes |

## Gameplay Systems Interaction

### Heavier Lift Mechanic

Some missions require:

- A 5-10% weight increase
- Or 2 extra reps per set

Warriors:

- Gain bonus XP for completing these
- Are less penalized for attempting higher weight

### Boss Synergy

Warriors excel in boss fights through:

- Higher final roll bonuses
- Strong performance scaling
- The ability to exceed final set targets

## Boss Mechanics Interaction

Warriors gain advantages when:

- Exceeding final set reps
- Maintaining high performance across sets
- Entering boss fights with high HP

### Example Boss Bonuses

- +2 on final boss roll for strong performance
- Additional bonus for heavy lifts

## Visual Evolution System

### Overview

The Warrior visually evolves as they level up in IronQuest.

This evolution is reflected in:

- AI-generated character portraits
- Equipped gear visuals
- Post-boss Victory Portraits

The goal is to create:

> "I can see my progress, not just track it."

### Evolution Tiers

Warrior visuals evolve across 5 major tiers:

| Tier   | Level Range | Identity      |
| ------ | ----------- | ------------- |
| Tier 1 | 1-2         | Unproven      |
| Tier 2 | 3-5         | Trained       |
| Tier 3 | 6-8         | Battle-Tested |
| Tier 4 | 9-12        | Elite         |
| Tier 5 | 13-15       | Mythic        |

### Tier 1 - Unproven

#### Visual Style

- Light armor or training gear
- Basic weapon such as a simple blade or hammer
- Clean, minimal detail

#### Tone

- Potential, not power
- Controlled stance

#### Pose Direction

- Neutral stance
- Slight readiness

#### Environment

- Training Grounds
- Soft lighting

### Tier 2 - Trained

#### Visual Style

- Basic armor pieces added
- Weapon looks more defined
- Slight wear marks

#### Tone

- Competent
- Gaining confidence

#### Pose Direction

- Ready stance
- Weapon visible

#### Environment

- First real locations such as Grim Hollow and the early world
- Light environmental effects such as fog and embers

### Tier 3 - Battle-Tested

#### Visual Style

- Full armor set, still grounded
- Noticeable battle wear
- Stronger weapon presence

#### Tone

- Experienced
- Hardened

#### Pose Direction

- Aggressive stance
- Mid-action or post-strike

#### Environment

- Active battle scenes
- Enemies or aftermath visible

### Tier 4 - Elite

#### Visual Style

- Enhanced armor with engravings and accents
- Larger, more imposing weapons
- Subtle glow or aura

#### Tone

- Dominant
- Commanding presence

#### Pose Direction

- Victorious stance
- Enemy defeated nearby

#### Environment

- Large-scale battle
- Fire, destruction, and dramatic lighting

### Tier 5 - Mythic

#### Visual Style

- Legendary armor with a unique silhouette
- Weapon feels iconic
- Visual effects such as energy, aura, or heat distortion

#### Tone

- Unstoppable
- Larger-than-life

#### Pose Direction

- Heroic and cinematic
- Elevated or central framing

#### Environment

- Epic battlefield or mythic setting
- Massive scale such as ruins, armies, and sky effects

## Gear Integration

Character visuals dynamically update based on:

- Equipped weapon
- Equipped armor
- Key artifacts

### Rules

- Weapon always visible in portrait
- Armor affects silhouette and bulk
- Accessories add subtle visual details such as glow or markings

### Trigger Moments

Generate updated portraits on:

Each portrait should show:

- Increased intensity
