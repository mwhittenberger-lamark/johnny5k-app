# Johnny5k: IronQuest - Skyreach Cliffs

## Location Role

Skyreach Cliffs ends the ranger group with vertical travel, balance, and exposure. It is the cleanest expression of movement-as-mastery.

## Boss Mission

### Mission 11: The Tempest Roc

#### Unlock Conditions

- Complete all 10 prior missions
- Have at least 102 HP
- Carry climbing and recovery supplies

#### Boss Narrative

> Its wings cast a shadow over the entire cliff face before the first strike lands.

## Metadata Completion

### Design Goals

- End the ranger arc with vertical mastery and exposure
- Make travel feel inseparable from danger

### Design Intent

- Reward balance, climbing confidence, and endurance under risk
- Make height itself part of the combat fantasy

### Boss Workout Mapping

- Full workout with repeated climb-and-recover phases
- Final set resolves the summit aerial confrontation
- Rewards late-session control and courage under exposure

### Boss Outcome Variants

| Outcome | Result                                                                 |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Victory | The upper aeries belong to the player and the sky route opens cleanly. |
| Partial | The roc yields the summit but still rules the outer winds.             |
| Failure | The storm and the wings force a retreat down the cliff path.           |
