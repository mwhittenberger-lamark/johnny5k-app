# Johnny5k: IronQuest

## Mage Spell List (All Subclasses) (v1)

---

# Overview

This document defines the complete Mage spell system in IronQuest.

Includes:

- All Mage-accessible spells (primarily Warlock + Mage-aligned utility)
- Minimum level requirements
- Gold costs
- Effects

Spells are designed to:

- enhance gameplay
- support class identity
- avoid breaking core fitness systems

---

# Spell Tiers

| Tier   | Level Range | Power                     |
| ------ | ----------- | ------------------------- |
| Tier 1 | 2–4         | Light utility             |
| Tier 2 | 5–7         | Moderate effects          |
| Tier 3 | 8–10        | Strong abilities          |
| Tier 4 | 11–15       | High-impact / limited use |

# TIER 1 — NOVICE SPELLS

- Level: 2
- Cost: 20 Gold
- Effect: +1 on one mission roll per day

## Shadow Veil

- Level: 2
- Cost: 20 Gold
- Effect: Reduce HP loss by 2 (once per mission)

## Flicker Step

- Level: 3
- Cost: 25 Gold
- Effect: Gain +1 Travel Point (once per day)

- Level: 3
- Cost: 25 Gold

## Grim Focus

- Level: 4
- Cost: 30 Gold

# EQUIP RULES

- Level 1–4: Equip 2 spells
- Level 5–9: Equip 3 spells
- Level 10+: Equip 4 spells

---

# DESIGN NOTES

- Early spells support learning and flexibility
- Mid-tier spells improve efficiency
- Late-tier spells impact boss fights and progression

---

# SUMMARY

Mage spells provide:

- control over outcomes
- recovery advantages
- strategic flexibility

They reinforce the Mage identity:

> “Master the system. Control the outcome.”
