## Boss Mission

### Mission 13: The Prime Engine

#### Unlock Conditions

- Complete all 12 prior missions
- Have at least 106 HP
- Carry tuned mechanical supplies

#### Boss Narrative

> The Citadel's heart does not scream. It ticks louder.

## Metadata Completion

### Design Goals

- Open the high-flavor arc with a strong rhythm identity
- Increase mission volume without losing clarity

### Design Intent

- Reward timing, pattern recognition, and steady output
- Make machine precision feel oppressive instead of clean

### Boss Workout Mapping

- Full workout built around timed phases and rhythm pressure
- Final set resolves the engine room collapse
- Rewards tempo control under mounting strain

### Boss Outcome Variants

| Outcome | Result                                                                     |
| ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Victory | The Citadel runs on your timing now and its systems fall into clean order. |
| Partial | The Engine slows, but whole sectors still grind against each other.        |
| Failure | The clockwork surges and the lower wards seal shut behind you.             |
