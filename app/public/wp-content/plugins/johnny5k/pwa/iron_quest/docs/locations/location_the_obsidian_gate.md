# Johnny5k: IronQuest - The Obsidian Gate

## Location Role

The Obsidian Gate turns escalation into atmosphere. Every mission feels like the world holding its breath while something worse pushes through.

## Overview

| Attribute   | Detail                                      |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Name        | The Obsidian Gate                           |
| Theme       | Dark portal, escalating threat              |
| Tone        | Apocalyptic, severe, shadow-lit and ominous |
| Level range | Levels 84-90                                |

### Short Lore

> The Gate was built to seal a war. It now remembers the war better than the people guarding it.

### Design Goals

- Make apocalyptic escalation feel immediate and real
- Turn a portal zone into a progression checkpoint, not just scenery

### Design Intent

- Use dread and breach pressure as the main emotional drivers
- Keep the player working against collapse itself

### Boss Narrative

> Behind the Gate, a shape steps forward wearing smoke like armor and daylight like an insult.

### Boss Workout Mapping

- Full workout with repeated breach surges and shrinking recovery windows
- Final set decides whether the Gate stays shut
- Rewards control under catastrophic pressure

### Boss Outcome Variants

| Outcome | Result                                                                       |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Victory | The Gate seals with a sound like a mountain deciding not to fall.            |
| Partial | The Harbinger is pushed back, but hairline cracks remain in the black glass. |
| Failure | The breach widens and the camps beyond the wall prepare for evacuation.      |
