# Johnny5k: IronQuest

## Subclass Abilities List (Non-Warlock) (v1)

This document defines progression-based purchasable or learnable abilities for the remaining subclasses in IronQuest.

Included:

- Warrior: Berserker, Knight, Gladiator
- Ranger: Scout, Hunter, Beastmaster
- Mage: Sorcerer, Cleric
- Rogue: Assassin, Thief, Shadow

The Warlock spell list is maintained separately.

---

# Global Design Rules

- Abilities unlock by subclass level
- Abilities may be:
  - learned automatically at milestone levels
  - purchased after reaching minimum level
- Effects should reinforce class identity without invalidating the core fitness systems
- Bonuses should be meaningful but moderate
- Stronger abilities should be limited to once per mission, once per day, or once per week

# WARRIOR SUBCLASSES

## Berserker

High risk, high reward. Best for users who like to push harder than prescribed.

### 1. Bloodrush

- **Minimum Berserker Level:** 2
- **Suggested Cost:** 20 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +1 on one over-target set once per day.
- **Use Case:** Early reward for pushing beyond reps.

### 2. Savage Followthrough

- **Minimum Berserker Level:** 3
- **Suggested Cost:** 25 Gold
- **Effect:** If you exceed reps on one set, gain +5 bonus XP after the mission.
- **Use Case:** Incentivizes extra reps.

### 3. Reckless Swing

- **Minimum Berserker Level:** 4
- **Suggested Cost:** 30 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +2 on a final boss roll, but take +3 HP loss during that mission.
- **Use Case:** Risk/reward burst.

### 4. Iron Rage

- **Minimum Berserker Level:** 5
- **Suggested Cost:** 40 Gold
- **Effect:** Ignore one failed heavy set penalty once per day.
- **Use Case:** Lets aggression stay viable.

### 5. Crimson Momentum

- **Minimum Berserker Level:** 7
- **Suggested Cost:** 55 Gold
- **Effect:** Consecutive successful heavy sets grant +1 total mission outcome bonus.
- **Use Case:** Rewards streaks.

# Optional Learning Model

You can apply one of these systems to all subclasses:

## Model A — Purchase Only

- Abilities unlock for purchase once minimum level is reached

## Model B — Hybrid

- Each subclass auto-learns one signature ability at milestone levels
- Additional abilities are purchased

### Suggested Auto-Learn Milestones

- Level 2: First signature ability
- Level 4: Core identity ability
- Level 8: Capstone starter ability

---

# Recommended Equip Limits

To prevent overload:

- Low levels: equip 2 abilities
- Mid levels: equip 3 abilities
- High levels: equip 4 abilities

This gives meaningful choice without forcing users to manage too many moving parts.

- Knight = defense and reliability
- Gladiator = momentum and streaks

- Scout = movement and travel
- Hunter = cardio-combat bridge

---

# Balance Notes

- Avoid passive stacking beyond a small capped total
- Strong weekly effects should feel powerful but not mandatory
- Accessibility subclasses should be genuinely viable, not “easy mode” in a dismissive way
- Gold-boosting abilities should not break the store economy
