# Johnny5k: IronQuest

## Warlock Spell List (v1)

This document defines a starter spell economy for the **Warlock** subclass in IronQuest, including:

- spell tiers
- minimum Warlock level required
- brief effects
- suggested gold purchase values

These spells are designed to feel magical and RPG-driven without overpowering the fitness gameplay loop.

---

# Design Rules

- Warlocks unlock stronger spells as they level up
- Spells may be **learned** automatically at milestone levels or **purchased** from shops / arcane vendors
- Spells should provide light to moderate gameplay influence
- Spells should not invalidate core systems like sleep, HP management, travel, or workout performance

---

# Spell Tiers

## Tier 1 — Novice Spells

Available early. Small but meaningful effects.

## Tier 2 — Adept Spells

More specialized utility and moderate bonuses.

## Tier 3 — Advanced Spells

Powerful but controlled effects tied to mission prep, recovery, or boss moments.

## Tier 4 — Master Spells

Rare, high-value spells with strong identity and limited usage.

---

# Tier 1 — Novice Spells

## 1. Ember Spark

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 2
- **Suggested Cost:** 20 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +1 on one standard mission outcome roll once per day.
- **Use Case:** Helpful early reliability boost.

## 2. Shadow Veil

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 2
- **Suggested Cost:** 20 Gold
- **Effect:** Reduce HP loss by 2 during one mission once per day.
- **Use Case:** Early survivability.

## 3. Flicker Step

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 3
- **Suggested Cost:** 25 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +1 Travel Point once per day.
- **Use Case:** Helps with movement across the map.

## 4. Bone Whisper

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 3
- **Suggested Cost:** 25 Gold
- **Effect:** Reveal a hint about the next mission or boss condition in the current location.
- **Use Case:** Planning and anticipation.

## 5. Grim Focus

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 4
- **Suggested Cost:** 30 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +1 on the final set of one workout once per day.
- **Use Case:** Finisher support.

---

# Tier 2 — Adept Spells

## 6. Siphon Vitality

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 5
- **Suggested Cost:** 40 Gold
- **Effect:** Restore 5 HP after completing a mission successfully.
- **Use Case:** Soft recovery tool.

## 7. Arcane Stride

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 5
- **Suggested Cost:** 40 Gold
- **Effect:** Convert one cardio session into +2 additional Travel Points.
- **Use Case:** Travel acceleration.

## 8. Hex of Burden

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 6
- **Suggested Cost:** 45 Gold
- **Effect:** Ignore one “heavier than normal” mission requirement once per day.
- **Use Case:** Accessibility and flexibility.

## 9. Rune of Preservation

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 6
- **Suggested Cost:** 45 Gold
- **Effect:** Prevent one failed set from triggering extra HP loss.
- **Use Case:** Boss or hard mission insurance.

## 10. Dark Ledger

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 7
- **Suggested Cost:** 50 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +10 bonus gold when completing a boss mission.
- **Use Case:** Economy scaling.

---

# Tier 3 — Advanced Spells

## 11. Infernal Reserve

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 8
- **Suggested Cost:** 65 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +1 Healing Potion at the start of the week.
- **Use Case:** Sustained progression support.

## 12. Wraithform

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 8
- **Suggested Cost:** 65 Gold
- **Effect:** Reduce total mission HP loss by 25% for one mission.
- **Use Case:** Difficult content survival.

## 13. Rift Skip

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 9
- **Suggested Cost:** 70 Gold
- **Effect:** Ignore the 50% fast-travel gold cap once per week.
- **Use Case:** Major travel flexibility.

## 14. Blood Sigil

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 9
- **Suggested Cost:** 70 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +2 on the final boss roll, but lose 5 HP after the mission regardless of outcome.
- **Use Case:** Risk/reward boss spell.

## 15. Grave Pact

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 10
- **Suggested Cost:** 75 Gold
- **Effect:** If a mission would drop you below 10 HP, remain at 10 HP once per week.
- **Use Case:** Safety net.

---

# Tier 4 — Master Spells

## 16. Crown of Ash

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 12
- **Suggested Cost:** 100 Gold
- **Effect:** Gain +2 on all boss progress gains during one boss mission.
- **Use Case:** High-end boss specialization.

## 17. Nightgate

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 12
- **Suggested Cost:** 100 Gold
- **Effect:** Instantly gain 3 Travel Points once per week.
- **Use Case:** Strategic world movement.

## 18. Soulbrand

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 14
- **Suggested Cost:** 120 Gold
- **Effect:** After a dominant boss victory, gain +20 bonus gold and +20 bonus XP.
- **Use Case:** Endgame snowball reward.

## 19. Void Lantern

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 14
- **Suggested Cost:** 120 Gold
- **Effect:** Reveal all remaining missions and boss traits in the current location.
- **Use Case:** Planning and mastery.

## 20. Pact Eternal

- **Minimum Warlock Level:** 15
- **Suggested Cost:** 150 Gold
- **Effect:** Once per week, fully restore HP after completing a boss mission.
- **Use Case:** Signature capstone spell.

# Optional Learning Model

You can use either of these systems:

## Model A — Purchase Only

- All spells must be bought once level requirement is met

## Model B — Hybrid

- Warlock automatically learns one signature spell at key levels
- Additional spells can be purchased

### Suggested Auto-Learn Milestones

- Level 2: Ember Spark
- Level 5: Siphon Vitality
- Level 8: Wraithform
- Level 12: Crown of Ash

---

# Recommended UI Grouping

In the app, spells could be grouped by:

- **Combat**
- **Travel**
- **Recovery**
- **Boss**
- **Utility**

This makes them easier for non-RPG users to understand.

---

# Balance Notes

- Limit active equipped spells if needed, such as:
  - **2 equipped spells at low levels**
  - **3 equipped spells at mid levels**
  - **4 equipped spells at high levels**
- Passive stacking should be capped to avoid overpowering other classes
- Stronger spells should have:
  - daily limits
  - weekly limits
  - tradeoffs when appropriate
