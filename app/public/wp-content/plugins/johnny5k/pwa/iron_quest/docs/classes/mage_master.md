# Johnny5k: IronQuest

## Mage Class - Master Design Document (v1)

## Overview

The **Mage** is the consistency and control-focused class in IronQuest.

Mages thrive on:

- Completing workouts
- Maintaining routines
- Recovery through sleep and HP management
- Steady, controlled performance

They are designed for users who:

- Want structure and balance
- Are building consistency habits
- Prefer reliability over intensity spikes

## Core Identity

> "Control the effort. Master the outcome."

## Primary Playstyle

- Complete full workouts consistently
- Prioritize recovery and sleep
- Perform steadily across sessions
- Reduce penalties from fatigue and mistakes

## Core Bonuses

| Bonus Type         | Effect                                      |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| Full workout bonus | +20 XP for full workout completion          |
| Sleep bonus        | +10 XP when sleep is logged                 |
| Streak bonus       | +5 XP per streak day, capped at +25         |
| Recovery advantage | Increased effectiveness of recovery systems |

## Base Stats

| Stat              | Value                             |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Max HP            | 100                               |

## Gameplay Systems Interaction

### Recovery System

Mages benefit most from:

- Sleep logging
- Potion usage
- Reduced HP penalties

### Heavier Lift Mechanic

Mages are not built around heavy-lift dominance, but they compensate through:

- Full-completion rewards
- Recovery loops
- Lower penalty impact from imperfect sessions

### Streak System

Mages gain bonuses from:

- Consecutive workout days
- Consistent routines
- Full completion

### Boss Synergy

Mages perform best when:

- Workouts are fully completed
- HP is well managed
- Consistency is maintained across sets

## Boss Mechanics Interaction

Mages gain advantages when:

- Reaching boss encounters with strong recovery
- Avoiding failed sets
- Completing full workouts cleanly

### Example Boss Bonuses

- +1 boss progress for full completion
- +2 final boss roll if the workout is fully completed

## Visual Evolution System

### Overview

Mages evolve visually through increasing magical mastery.

This evolution is reflected in:

- AI-generated character portraits
- Equipped gear visuals
- Post-boss Victory Portraits

The goal is to create:

> "My control looks stronger, not just my numbers."

### Evolution Reflection Points

- Stronger magical glow
- More complex robes and symbols
- Increased environmental magic presence

### Evolution Goal

- Make consistency and mastery feel visually dramatic over time

### Evolution Tiers

| Tier   | Level Range | Identity    |
| ------ | ----------- | ----------- |
| Tier 1 | 1-2         | Initiate    |
| Tier 2 | 3-5         | Apprentice  |
| Tier 3 | 6-8         | Adept       |
| Tier 4 | 9-12        | Archmage    |
| Tier 5 | 13-15       | Mythic Mage |

### Tier 1 - Initiate

#### Identity

- Untrained but promising
- First signs of control

#### Visual Style

- Simple robes
- Minimal magical ornamentation
- Small, controlled glow

#### Tone

- Careful
- Curious

#### Pose Direction

- Calm stance
- Hands prepared but restrained

#### Environment

- Training or study spaces
- Soft magical lighting

### Tier 2 - Apprentice

#### Identity

- Competent learner
- Routine-building practitioner

#### Visual Style

- Stronger robe detail
- Early magical symbols
- Slightly brighter effects

#### Tone

- Focused
- Growing in confidence

#### Pose Direction

- Controlled casting posture
- Stable and composed

#### Environment

- Early towers, archives, and controlled magical spaces

### Tier 3 - Adept

#### Identity

- Reliable practitioner
- Controlled field mage

#### Visual Style

- Visible energy effects
- Layered garments
- Better magical tools

#### Tone

- Skilled
- Deliberate

#### Pose Direction

- Casting or command pose
- Energy visibly shaped rather than explosive

#### Environment

- Active magical scenes
- Light distortion or controlled aura effects

### Tier 4 - Archmage

#### Identity

- High-control spell authority
- Dominant magical presence

#### Visual Style

- Elaborate robes
- Strong aura
- Arcane insignia and effects

#### Tone

- Powerful
- Composed

#### Pose Direction

- Dominant but restrained
- Heroic upper-body framing

#### Environment

- Spires, sanctums, or astral settings
- Strong magical architecture

### Tier 5 - Mythic Mage

#### Identity

- Reality-shaping master
- Legendary controller

#### Visual Style

- Mythic robes
- Reality-warping magical effects
- Iconic magical silhouette

#### Tone

- Transcendent
- Larger-than-life

#### Pose Direction

- Cinematic command pose
- Centered, controlled, inevitable

#### Environment

- Epic magical landscapes
